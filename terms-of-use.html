2. What the App does

PatternPlot lets you trace paper sewing patterns into editable, true-scale vector pieces; add seam allowance, notches, and grainlines; deterministically grade a base size into a run of sizes; and export true-scale tiled and A0 PDFs. Grading geometry is computed deterministically on your device.

The App flags when a size drifts beyond a safe grade range, but you must verify graded patterns — and print a test / calibration square at 100% scale — before cutting fabric or putting a pattern into production. We are not liable for wasted material or production loss arising from unverified output.

AI-assisted suggestions are advisory only. They never set your measurements or geometry: every suggestion is re-validated on your device, and the deterministic engine remains the source of truth for every coordinate, seam allowance, notch, and graded measurement.
